# ToyTopia -  Local Toy Store Online

A colorful website where parents can find awesome toys from local sellers. Browse toys, check reviews, and discover perfect gifts for kids!

##  ToyTopia Motivation
I wanted to create something that helps families find great toys while supporting small local businesses. Parents can easily browse through different toys, see all the details they need, and even submit requests to try toys before buying with policy. It's basically bringing the local toy store experience online!

##  What Makes It Nice

- **Easy Sign Up** - Register with your email  or just click to sign in with Google
- **Browse Tons of Toys** - Beautiful image gallery showing all available toys with prices and ratings
- **Protected Features** - Some pages require login to keep your info safe
- **Update Your Profile** - Change your name and profile picture anytime you want

- **Find Local Stores** - See all toy sellers in your area with their contact info
- **Password Reset** - Forgot your password? No problem, reset it via email
- **Responsiveness** - Looks great on phones, tablets, and computers

##  What Website Used

- **react** - Main library for building everything
- **react-router-dom** - Handles switching between pages
- **firebase** - Takes care of user login and authentication
- **react-icons** - All the icons you see on the site
- **swiper** - Makes the image slider smooth and nice
- **react-hot-toast** - Shows those popup notifications
- **tailwindcss** - Made styling super easy
- **daisyui** - Ready-made components that look professional

##  Firebase 

Create a `.env.local` file in your project root and add:
```
VITE_APIKEY=your_firebase_api_key
VITE_AUTHDOMAIN=your_auth_domain
VITE_PROJECTID=your_project_id
VITE_STORAGEBUCKET=your_storage_bucket
VITE_MESSAGINGSENDERID=your_sender_id
VITE_APPID=your_app_id
```

##  All Pages

- **Home** - Landing page with featured toys and slider
- **All Products** - Complete catalog of available toys (login required)
- **Toy Details** - Full information about each toy (login required)
- **Store Locations** - Find local sellers near you
- **My Profile** - View and edit your account details (login required)
- **Login/Register** - Create account or sign in
- **Forgot Password** - Reset your password easily
- **404** - Fun error page if you get lost

 
##  Made With

React, Firebase, Tailwind CSS, DaisyUI.
